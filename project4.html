System Structure

In order to manage the size and complexity of our design system, the system is divided into three libraries: Color & Typography Library, Iconography Library, and Component Library. The first two libraries hold Design Tokens used in the largest part of our design system, our Component Library. Splitting up the three libraries of the system decreases loading times and network strain caused by having one massive library.

What are Design Tokens?

Design Tokens are the single source of truth to name and store a design decision.

System Libraries

Color & Typography Library:

I started with the Color & Typography Library to create the base for our Design System. This library holds Color and Typography Design Tokens; used throughout libraries, designs, and in development to maintain design consistency.

Color Design Tokens for both light and dark modes verified WCAG passing contrast ratios. This aids in design work and maintaining accessibility in our products, ensuring that designs are accessible without relying on the designer or developer to verify accessibility.

The Color & Typography Library also contains documentation notes on how these Color and Typography Design Tokens should be used.
