#DailyUI

I am currently designing my way through a 100 day, daily UI challenge to become a better designer and design-oriented thinker. I will be updating my progess on here frequently but for daily updates and feedback connect with me on Linkedin.

Connect on Linkedin

#001
Sign Up Screen

Day one, I have always liked space and so I wanted to do a few designs around that and this is the first; a sign up screen for a mock space program.

#002
Credit Card Checkout

Day two, most of the credit card forms out there are very plain and monotonous so I wanted to go beyond that and design a credit one that was a little more fun and colorful.

#003
Landing Page

Day three, I decided to switch things up and do a desktop mockup instead of mobile for today's challenge of designing a landing page and I wanted to cover a subject very important to me: saving our ocean.

#004
Calculator

Day four was a fun challenge that required me to look at something I have seen a thousand times before and put my own spin on it.

#005
App Icon

Day five, I wanted to challenge myself and create an app icon entirely from scratch with no pre-made elements. So I made an icon for a mountain biking brand using elements created individually created by me (including the wheel).

#006
User Profile

Day six asked me to design a user profile. I decided to take it in the direction of a mobile social platorm for finding and connecting photographers and customers with potential models for shoots and other model work.

#007
Settings

Day seven, I designed a settings page for a concept pool management application.

#008
404 Page

Nobody likes seeing the 404 page but when a site has a funny/corny 404 page it makes the experience a little less upsetting. So for challenge day eight, I created my own./p>

#009
Media Player

I initially started designing a mobile-only player but I wanted to challenge myself to create two different variations of it for both mobile and desktop. Overall this was one of the most challenging days of the challenge so far but I am excited to see what is to come!

#010
Social Sharing

I wanted to go beyond just creating a set of static images showing one or two icon states and try my hand at animation for the button sequence. It ended up being more challenging than I anticipated but once I got going it all fell into place. Overall this was a fun challenge and I am very happy with the results.

#011
Flash Message

After the last challenge, I wanted to try my hand at animating again and I got the opportunity the next day with a challenge to design a flash message that displays an error or success. I knew I wanted to animate a button featuring a loading sequence but beyond the typical spinning circle. So I created an enter button and loading sequence that finishes with either a green success or red failure display.

#012
E-Commerce

I wanted to do something a little different than a traditional e-commerce page, so I went for a minimalist and modern feel using white-space and bold color.

#013
Direct Message

Day 13; I want to further challenge myself and create beautiful and accessible designs for every project I do. Even for something small, such as challenge like this, because the internet is a place for everyone and its about time it was designed that way. Because of this, the font used in this project is OpenDyslexic; a font designed to enhance readability for dyselxic users like me (read more about the OpenDyslexic font here).
