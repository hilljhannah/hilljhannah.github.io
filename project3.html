#DailyUI

I am currently designing my way through a 100 day, daily UI challenge to become a better designer and design-oriented thinker. I will be updating my progess on here frequently but for daily updates and feedback connect with me on Linkedin.

Connect on Linkedin

#001
Sign Up Screen

Day one, I have always liked space and so I wanted to do a few designs around that and this is the first; a sign up screen for a mock space program.

#002
Credit Card Checkout

Day two, most of the credit card forms out there are very plain and monotonous so I wanted to go beyond that and design a credit one that was a little more fun and colorful.

#003
Landing Page

Day three, I decided to switch things up and do a desktop mockup instead of mobile for today's challenge of designing a landing page and I wanted to cover a subject very important to me: saving our ocean.

#004
Calculator

Day four was a fun challenge that required me to look at something I have seen a thousand times before and put my own spin on it.

#005
App Icon

Day five, I wanted to challenge myself and create an app icon entirely from scratch with no pre-made elements. So I made an icon for a mountain biking brand using elements created individually created by me (including the wheel).

#006
User Profile

Day six asked me to design a user profile. I decided to take it in the direction of a mobile social platorm for finding and connecting photographers and customers with potential models for shoots and other model work.

#007
Settings

Day seven, I designed a settings page for a concept pool management application.
